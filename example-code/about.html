About Amber‑Modern

Amber‑Modern is dedicated to offering exceptional staging and interior design services in Flagstaff, AZ, while actively reducing waste that contributes to landfills and ocean pollution.

Our staging services primarily utilize second‑hand items sourced from trusted marketplaces. Each piece is inspected and refurbished so it looks brand new—delivering luxury without the environmental costs of mass production.
