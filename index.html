About Amber-Modern

We understand how overwhelming it can be to feel your home or business is not showcasing its full potential.

With years of experience in staging and interior design, weve transformed countless spaces with stunning furnishings.

Why Amber-Modern?

At Amber-Modern, we will create an atmosphere that accentuates your homes unique features and help potential buyers to envision their future lives here.

By investing in staging and interior design services, your home is likely to sell quicker, receive higher offers, and overall, gain more traffic compared to homes without staging.

Additionally, we are dedicated to environmental sustainability by choosing beautiful, pre-owned and refurbished furniture pieces from homes and businesses.

A Wider Range of Services

Services

An Environmentally Friendly Approach

Amber-Modern is committed to sustainability by incorporating furniture and decor which has been refurbished and pre-owned, aiming to minimize the amount of furniture and waste which ends up in our landfills each year.
